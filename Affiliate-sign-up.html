Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our affiliate program and application process.

How long does the application process take?
We typically review and approve applications within 24 hours. In some cases, it might take up to 48 hours if we need additional information. Once approved, you'll get immediate access to your affiliate dashboard.
Do I need a website to join?
While having a website or blog is helpful, it's not required. We accept affiliates who promote through social media, YouTube, email lists, or other channels. What matters most is that you have an audience interested in document or image tools.
When and how do I get paid?
We process affiliate payments monthly around the 15th of each month for the previous month's commissions. Payments are made via your preferred method (PayPal, bank transfer, etc.). The minimum payout threshold is $50.
What is your cookie duration?
We use a 90-day cookie duration. This means if someone clicks your affiliate link and makes a purchase within 90 days, you'll receive credit for that sale.
Can I promote Photo Flare on multiple channels?
Absolutely! We encourage promotion across all your channels - website, social media, email newsletters, YouTube, etc. The more places you promote, the more potential customers you can reach.

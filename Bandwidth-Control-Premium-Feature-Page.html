Frequently Asked Questions

Why would I need bandwidth control for cloud sync?
Bandwidth control is essential for managing your network resources effectively. Large cloud sync operations can consume significant bandwidth, potentially slowing down other important activities like video calls, streaming, or online gaming. With bandwidth control, you can set limits to ensure your sync operations don't interfere with your other online activities.
How does scheduled bandwidth work?
Scheduled bandwidth allows you to set different speed limits for different times of day. For example, you might set lower limits during work hours (9AM-5PM) to ensure your work applications have priority, moderate limits in the evening for general use, and unlimited or high limits overnight when network usage is typically lower. This automation ensures optimal performance without manual intervention.
Can I set different limits for upload and download?
Yes, Photo Flare Premium allows you to set separate limits for upload and download speeds. This is particularly useful because many internet connections have asymmetric speeds (faster download than upload). You can optimize these settings based on your specific internet plan and usage patterns.
Will bandwidth control slow down my sync operations?
Bandwidth control will limit the maximum speed of your sync operations, but this is intentional and beneficial. While individual syncs might take slightly longer, your overall productivity will improve because other internet activities won't be impacted. You can always temporarily disable limits when you need maximum sync speed for urgent transfers.
Is there a free trial for bandwidth control features?
Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial for our Premium plan, which includes full access to all bandwidth control features and settings. No credit card is required to start the trial, so you can test how these features improve your workflow before deciding to upgrade.

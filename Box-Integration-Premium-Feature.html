Frequently Asked Questions

How does Box integration work with Photo Flare?
Our Box integration uses secure OAuth authentication to connect your Box account directly to Photo Flare. Once connected, you can browse, manage, and transfer files between Box and other connected cloud storage services without downloading them to your device.
Is my data secure when using Box integration?
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security protocols. Your files are never stored on our servers during transfer, and we only request the minimum permissions needed to provide the integration features. Box's enterprise-grade security remains intact.
Can I transfer files between Box and other cloud services?
Absolutely! With Photo Flare Premium, you can transfer files directly between Box and other connected cloud storage providers like Google Drive, Dropbox, and OneDrive without having to download them to your computer first.
Do you support Box's collaboration features?
While our integration focuses on file management and transfer, we maintain Box's collaboration features. Comments, version history, and sharing settings are preserved when files are accessed through Photo Flare.
Is there a free trial for Box integration?
Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial for our Premium plan, which includes full access to Box integration and all other premium features. No credit card is required to start the trial.

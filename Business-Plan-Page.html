Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the Business plan.

How many team members can I add to the Business plan?
The Business plan includes up to 5 team members by default. For larger teams, we offer custom enterprise pricing. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific needs and get a tailored quote.
Is there a free trial for the Business plan?
Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for the Business plan. No credit card is required to start the trial, and you can cancel at any time during the trial period without being charged.
Can I upgrade from Pro to Business?
Absolutely! You can upgrade from Pro to Business at any time. When you upgrade, you'll immediately gain access to all Business features, and your billing cycle will be prorated based on your remaining Pro subscription.
Do you offer volume discounts for larger teams?
Yes, we offer custom pricing for teams with more than 10 members. Contact our sales team to discuss volume discounts and enterprise features tailored to your organization's needs.
What kind of dedicated support is included?
Business plan customers receive priority support with guaranteed response times, a dedicated account manager, and access to premium support channels including phone support during business hours.

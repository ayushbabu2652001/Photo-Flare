We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and in some regions, Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your premium features will remain active until the end of your billing period.
Is there a free trial for premium features?
We offer a 7-day free trial for our Pro plan. No credit card required to start the trial.
What happens if I exceed my usage limits on the free plan?
You'll be prompted to upgrade to continue using the service. Your files and data will be saved, and you can pick up where you left off after upgrading.

Yes, we offer a 50% discount for students and verified non-profit organizations. Please contact our support team with proof of eligibility.

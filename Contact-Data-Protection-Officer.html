Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Data Protection Officer?
A Data Protection Officer (DPO) is an enterprise security leadership role required by the GDPR. The DPO is responsible for overseeing data protection strategy and implementation to ensure compliance with GDPR requirements.
When should I contact the DPO instead of regular support?
Contact our DPO for matters related to data protection rights, privacy concerns, data processing inquiries, or if you believe your data privacy rights have been violated. For technical issues or general questions about our services, please contact our regular support team.
What information should I include in my message?
Please provide your full name, contact information, and a clear description of your inquiry. For data rights requests, specify which right you wish to exercise (access, correction, deletion, etc.) and any relevant account information.
How long does it take to get a response?
We aim to respond to all DPO inquiries within 3 business days. For data subject requests, we will acknowledge receipt within 24 hours and provide a complete response within 30 days as required by GDPR.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy Rights

We take your data privacy seriously and are committed to responding to all inquiries in a timely manner. Your privacy rights matter to us, and we will handle your request with the utmost care and attention.

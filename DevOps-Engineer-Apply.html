DevOps Engineer

Build and maintain our cloud infrastructure, ensuring high availability and performance for millions of users.

DevOps Engineer

Engineering Remote Full-time
Apply Now

Join our infrastructure team to build and scale the cloud platform that powers document processing for millions of users. You'll design, implement, and maintain our infrastructure to ensure high availability, performance, and security as we continue to grow.

About the Role

As a DevOps Engineer at Photo Flare, you'll be responsible for designing, building, and maintaining our cloud infrastructure. You'll work on everything from CI/CD pipelines to monitoring systems, ensuring our platform can handle millions of requests while maintaining reliability and performance.

Our infrastructure stack includes AWS, Kubernetes, Terraform, and a variety of monitoring and logging tools. You'll have the opportunity to work on challenging scalability problems and contribute to our infrastructure as we continue to grow our user base.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design, implement, and maintain cloud infrastructure on AWS
  • Manage Kubernetes clusters and containerized applications
  • Develop and maintain CI/CD pipelines for automated deployments
  • Implement infrastructure as code using Terraform and Ansible
  • Monitor system performance and implement improvements
  • Ensure high availability and disaster recovery capabilities
  • Implement and maintain security best practices
  • Collaborate with development teams on application deployment
  • Troubleshoot and resolve production infrastructure issues
  • Participate in on-call rotation for production support

Qualifications & Requirements

  • 3+ years of experience in DevOps or SRE roles
  • Strong experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
  • Proficiency with containerization (Docker) and orchestration (Kubernetes)
  • Experience with infrastructure as code (Terraform, CloudFormation)
  • Knowledge of CI/CD tools and practices
  • Experience with monitoring and logging tools
  • Understanding of networking and security principles
  • Scripting skills (Bash, Python, or similar)
  • Experience with Linux system administration
  • Strong problem-solving and communication skills

Bonus Points For

  • AWS certifications (Solutions Architect, DevOps Engineer, etc.)
  • Experience with service mesh technologies (Istio, Linkerd)
  • Knowledge of database administration and optimization
  • Experience with multi-region or multi-cloud deployments
  • Background in security or compliance (SOC2, GDPR, etc.)
  • Experience with performance tuning and capacity planning
  • Contributions to open source projects

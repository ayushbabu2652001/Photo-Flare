Growth Marketing Manager

Growth Marketing Manager

Marketing Full-time
Apply Now

Join our marketing team to drive the growth of Photo Flare's user base and revenue. You'll develop and execute data-driven marketing strategies that acquire, engage, and retain users across our product portfolio. We're looking for a creative, analytical marketer who can identify growth opportunities and scale them effectively.

About the Role

As a Growth Marketing Manager at Photo Flare, you'll be responsible for developing and executing strategies to drive user acquisition, engagement, and retention. You'll work across multiple channels to identify growth opportunities, run experiments, and scale successful initiatives. Your work will directly impact our user growth and revenue targets.

You'll collaborate with product, design, and engineering teams to create seamless user experiences that drive conversion and retention. We're looking for someone who is equally comfortable analyzing data and developing creative marketing campaigns.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and execute growth marketing strategies across multiple channels
  • Manage paid acquisition campaigns (PPC, social, display) with a focus on ROI
  • Optimize conversion funnels and user onboarding experiences
  • Plan and execute email marketing campaigns to drive engagement and retention
  • Collaborate with content team to develop SEO-optimized content
  • Analyze performance data to identify growth opportunities and optimize campaigns
  • Manage marketing budget and allocate resources effectively
  • Work with product team to implement growth-focused features
  • Develop and track KPIs to measure campaign effectiveness

Qualifications & Requirements

  • 4+ years of experience in growth marketing or performance marketing
  • Proven track record of driving user acquisition and revenue growth
  • Experience with paid acquisition channels (Google Ads, Facebook, etc.)
  • Strong analytical skills with experience in data analysis tools
  • Knowledge of SEO best practices and content marketing
  • Experience with marketing automation and CRM platforms
  • Familiarity with A/B testing and conversion rate optimization
  • Excellent communication and project management skills
  • Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Business, or related field

Bonus Points For

  • Experience in SaaS or software products
  • Knowledge of analytics tools (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Experience with marketing automation platforms
  • Background in content strategy or creation
  • Experience with affiliate or partnership marketing
  • Knowledge of international marketing strategies

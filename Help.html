Frequently Asked Questions

How do I merge multiple PDF files?

To merge PDF files, navigate to the "Merge PDF" tool from the PDF Tools section. Click "Add Files" to select the PDFs you want to merge. You can rearrange the files by dragging them. Once you're satisfied with the order, click "Merge PDF" and your combined file will be ready for download.

Our free plan allows merging up to 5 PDF files at once, while premium users can merge up to 50 files simultaneously.

What file formats can I convert my images to?

Photo Flare supports conversion between all major image formats including JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP, TIFF, WEBP, and SVG. You can convert images using our "Convert Image" tool in the Image Tools section.

Premium users have access to additional formats and batch conversion features.

Is there a file size limit for free users?

Yes, free users have a file size limit of 10MB per file. This applies to both PDF and image files. Premium users enjoy significantly higher limits:

  • Pro Monthly: 100MB per file
  • Pro Yearly: 500MB per file

If you need to process larger files occasionally, consider upgrading to a premium plan.

How does the AI Image Enhancer work?

Our AI Image Enhancer uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze and improve your images. It can:

  • Increase resolution without losing quality
  • Reduce noise and artifacts
  • Enhance colors and contrast
  • Sharpen blurry images

This is a premium feature available to Pro Monthly and Pro Yearly subscribers. You can access it from the Premium Tools section.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. For monthly subscriptions, cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. For yearly subscriptions, you'll retain access until the end of your paid period.

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied with our service, contact our support team within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.

Security & Privacy at Photo Flare

Learn how we protect your files and ensure your data remains private and secure throughout our document processing workflow.

Our Security Framework

Local Processing

All files are processed directly in your browser using advanced WebAssembly technology. Your data never leaves your computer, ensuring maximum privacy.

End-to-End Encryption

When files are uploaded, they're encrypted using AES-256 encryption, the same standard used by banks and government agencies.

Automatic File Deletion

All processed files are automatically deleted from our servers within 1 hour. For premium users, files are deleted immediately after processing.

No Human Access

Our systems are fully automated with no manual intervention. No one at Photo Flare can access or view your files during processing.

Detailed Security Measures

Secure Infrastructure

Our servers are hosted in ISO 27001 certified data centers with 24/7 monitoring, redundant power, and advanced fire suppression systems.

Access Controls

Strict access controls with multi-factor authentication for all employees. Regular security training and background checks for personnel with system access.

Malware Protection

All uploaded files are scanned for malware and viruses using multiple detection engines before processing begins.

Network Security

Advanced DDoS protection, Web Application Firewall (WAF), and regular penetration testing to identify and fix vulnerabilities.

Compliance

Our practices comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other major privacy regulations. We undergo regular third-party security audits.

Audit Logging

Comprehensive audit trails for all system activities with immutable logs to track and monitor access to our infrastructure.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where are my files stored during processing?

For basic processing, files are handled entirely in your browser and never leave your device. For more complex operations requiring server processing, files are temporarily stored in our secure, encrypted servers and automatically deleted within 1 hour of processing completion.

Can Photo Flare employees access my files?

No. Our systems are fully automated with strict access controls. No employee has the ability to view or access your files during processing. We've designed our architecture to ensure complete privacy through automated workflows.

How do you ensure data isn't intercepted during transfer?

All data transfers between your browser and our servers are encrypted using TLS 1.3, the latest and most secure version of the protocol. This ensures that even if data is intercepted, it cannot be read without the encryption keys.

What happens if there's a security breach?

We have a comprehensive incident response plan that includes immediate notification to affected users, regulatory authorities where required, and transparent communication about the nature and scope of any incident. Our architecture is designed to minimize the impact of any potential breach through data minimization and encryption.

Do you comply with privacy regulations like GDPR?

Yes, we're fully compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and other major privacy regulations. We provide tools for users to exercise their rights, including data access, correction, and deletion. Our Privacy Policy details our compliance measures.

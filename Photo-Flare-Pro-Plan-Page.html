Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial for the Pro plan?
Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial for the Pro plan. No credit card is required to start the trial, and you can cancel at any time during the trial period without being charged.
Can I upgrade from Free to Pro?
Absolutely! You can upgrade from the Free plan to Pro at any time. When you upgrade, you'll immediately gain access to all Pro features, and your billing cycle will start from that day.
What happens if I exceed the 100MB file size limit?
If you need to process files larger than 100MB, you'll need to upgrade to the Business plan, which supports files up to 500MB. Alternatively, you can split your large files into smaller parts.
Can I use the Pro plan for commercial purposes?
Yes, the Pro plan is designed for both personal and commercial use. You can use it for your business, freelance work, or any commercial projects without any restrictions.
Do you offer discounts for teams or educational institutions?
Yes, we offer special pricing for teams of 5 or more users and for verified educational institutions. Contact our sales team for more information about these discounts.

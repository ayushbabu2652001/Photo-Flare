Product Designer

Create intuitive, user-centered designs for our document tools, from concept to implementation.

Product Designer

Design Remote Full-time
Apply Now

Join our design team to create beautiful, intuitive experiences for our document processing tools. You'll work closely with product managers and engineers to translate complex workflows into simple, elegant interfaces that millions of users rely on every day.

About the Role

As a Product Designer at Photo Flare, you'll be responsible for the end-to-end design of our document processing tools. You'll conduct user research, create wireframes and prototypes, and work with engineers to implement your designs. You'll have a significant impact on our product direction and help shape the future of document processing.

Our design team values collaboration, user empathy, and attention to detail. We're looking for someone who can balance user needs with business goals and technical constraints to create solutions that are both beautiful and functional.

Our Design Process

1

Research

Understand user needs and business requirements

2

Ideate

Explore multiple solutions and create concepts

3

Design

Create detailed designs and prototypes

4

Validate

Test with users and iterate based on feedback

Our Design Tools

Figma Sketch Adobe Creative Suite Principle InVision Miro UserTesting Hotjar

Key Responsibilities

  • Design user flows, wireframes, and high-fidelity mockups for new features
  • Create interactive prototypes to test and validate design concepts
  • Conduct user research and usability testing to inform design decisions
  • Collaborate with product managers to define product requirements
  • Work closely with engineers to ensure designs are implemented accurately
  • Contribute to and maintain our design system
  • Present design work to stakeholders and gather feedback
  • Stay current with design trends, tools, and best practices

Qualifications & Requirements

  • 3+ years of product design experience
  • Strong portfolio demonstrating user-centered design solutions
  • Proficiency with design tools like Figma, Sketch, or Adobe XD
  • Experience conducting user research and usability testing
  • Understanding of information architecture and interaction design
  • Knowledge of responsive design principles
  • Excellent visual design skills with attention to typography and layout
  • Strong communication and collaboration skills
  • Ability to work effectively in a remote environment

Bonus Points For

  • Experience designing for productivity or document processing tools
  • Knowledge of front-end development (HTML/CSS/JavaScript)
  • Experience with data visualization design
  • Motion design or illustration skills
  • Experience working in agile development environments
  • Background in UX writing or content strategy

Portfolio Requirement

Please include a link to your portfolio in your application. We're particularly interested in seeing case studies that demonstrate your design process and problem-solving skills.

Your portfolio should showcase at least 2-3 projects with explanations of your role, the design challenges you faced, and how you arrived at your solutions.

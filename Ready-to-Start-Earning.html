Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our affiliate program.

How long does the approval process take?
We typically review and approve applications within 24-48 hours. During peak times, it might take up to 72 hours. You'll receive an email notification once your application has been reviewed.
When and how do I get paid?
Commissions are paid monthly around the 15th of each month for the previous month's earnings. We pay via PayPal, and there's a $50 minimum payout threshold. All payments are processed automatically.
Do I need a website to join?
While having a website or blog is beneficial, it's not required. We accept affiliates who promote through social media, YouTube, email lists, or other channels. What matters most is your ability to refer qualified customers.
How are the recurring commissions calculated?
You earn a commission based on the subscription price of each customer you refer. This commission continues for as long as the customer remains subscribed. If they upgrade or downgrade their plan, your commission adjusts accordingly.

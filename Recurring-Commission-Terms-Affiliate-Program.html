Recurring Commission Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: November 15, 2023

Important Note

All commissions in the Photo Flare Affiliate Program are recurring for the lifetime of the referred customer's subscription. This document outlines the complete terms and conditions governing our recurring commission structure.

1. Commission Structure

  • Lifetime Recurring Commissions: Affiliates earn commissions for as long as the referred customer maintains an active subscription with Photo Flare.
  • Tier-Based Rates: Commission rates are determined by your performance tier (Starter: 20%, Professional: 25%, Elite: 30%).
  • Tier Evaluation: Your commission tier is evaluated monthly based on the number of successful referrals in the previous month.
  • Commission Calculation: Commissions are calculated based on the subscription price at the time of payment, excluding any taxes or additional fees.

2. Eligibility & Qualification

  • Qualified Referrals: Only customers who sign up through your unique affiliate link and maintain an active paid subscription qualify for recurring commissions.
  • Cookie Duration: The referral tracking cookie lasts for 90 days. If a customer signs up within this period, you'll receive credit.
  • Self-Referrals: Affiliates cannot refer themselves or immediate family members to earn commissions.
  • Fraud Prevention: We reserve the right to withhold commissions from referrals that appear fraudulent or violate our terms of service.

3. Payment Terms

  • Payment Schedule: Commissions are paid monthly, around the 15th of each month for the previous month's earnings.
  • Minimum Payout: A minimum balance of $50 is required for payment. Unpaid balances roll over to subsequent months.
  • Payment Methods: We support payments via PayPal, bank transfer, and Wise. Additional fees may apply for certain payment methods.
  • Tax Documentation: Affiliates are responsible for providing necessary tax documentation if required by law.

4. Subscription Changes & Cancellations

  • Subscription Upgrades/Downgrades: Your commission adjusts automatically based on the customer's current subscription level.
  • Subscription Cancellations: If a customer cancels their subscription, your recurring commissions for that customer cease immediately.
  • Subscription Pauses: During subscription pauses, commissions are temporarily suspended until the subscription resumes.
  • Refunds: If a customer receives a refund, corresponding commissions will be deducted from your future earnings.

5. Program Changes & Termination

  • Program Modifications: Photo Flare reserves the right to modify the affiliate program terms, commission rates, or payment structure with 30 days' notice.
  • Grandfathering: Changes to commission rates typically apply prospectively, but we reserve the right to adjust rates for existing referrals if necessary.
  • Termination: Either party may terminate the affiliate relationship at any time with written notice.
  • Post-Termination Commissions: After termination, you will continue to receive commissions for existing qualified referrals for up to 90 days.

6. Marketing Guidelines

  • Brand Compliance: All marketing materials must comply with our brand guidelines and cannot misrepresent Photo Flare products or services.
  • Prohibited Practices: Spam, false advertising, trademark bidding, or any deceptive marketing practices are strictly prohibited.
  • Disclosure Requirements: You must clearly disclose your affiliate relationship with Photo Flare in accordance with FTC guidelines.
  • Content Usage: You may use official Photo Flare marketing materials but cannot modify them without prior written approval.

Need Clarification?

If you have any questions about these terms or need clarification on any aspect of our recurring commission structure, please contact our affiliate team at affiliates@photoflare.com.

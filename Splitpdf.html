About This Tool

How to Split PDF Files

  1. Click "Browse Files" or drag and drop a PDF file into the upload area
  2. Choose your preferred splitting method
  3. Configure the splitting options based on your selection
  4. Click "Split PDF" to divide your file
  5. Download your split PDF files individually or as a ZIP archive

Split Methods

  • Page Ranges: Split by specific page ranges (e.g., 1-5, 8, 11-13)
  • Single Pages: Extract individual pages as separate files
  • Every N Pages: Split after every specified number of pages

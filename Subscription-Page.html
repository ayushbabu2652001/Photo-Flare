Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay for monthly and annual subscriptions.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access to Pro features until the end of your billing period.
Is there a free trial for Pro features?
We don't offer a free trial for Pro features, but we do have a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund your payment.
What happens if I exceed the file size limit?
If you try to process a file larger than your plan's limit, you'll be prompted to upgrade to a higher plan. Free users have a 10MB limit, Pro Monthly users have a 500MB limit, and Pro Annual users have a 1GB limit.

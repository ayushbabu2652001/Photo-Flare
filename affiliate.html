Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our affiliate program.

How much can I earn as a Photo Flare affiliate?
Our commission structure starts at 20% and goes up to 30% for top performers. With our recurring commission model, you earn month after month for every customer you refer. Many of our affiliates earn hundreds to thousands of dollars per month.
How and when do I get paid?
We process affiliate payments monthly around the 15th of each month for the previous month's commissions. Payments are made via PayPal, bank transfer, or other methods depending on your location. The minimum payout threshold is $50.
What is your cookie duration?
We use a 90-day cookie duration. This means if someone clicks your affiliate link and makes a purchase within 90 days, you'll receive credit for that sale.
Can I promote Photo Flare on social media?
Absolutely! We encourage promotion on all social media platforms. We provide social media-friendly banners, pre-written posts, and other assets to make promotion easy and effective.
Is there a cost to join the affiliate program?
No, joining our affiliate program is completely free. There are no hidden fees or charges. We believe in creating a mutually beneficial partnership with our affiliates.

Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?
We support JPG, PNG, and WebP formats. The maximum file size is 10MB for free users and 100MB for Pro users.
How does the AI enhancement work?
Our AI uses deep learning models trained on millions of images. It analyzes your image content and applies targeted enhancements to improve resolution, colors, and details while reducing noise.
Is my data secure?
Yes, all image processing happens securely on our servers. We do not store your images after processing, and we use encryption to protect your data during transfer.
Can I use this for commercial purposes?
Yes, you can use enhanced images for commercial purposes. However, please ensure you have the rights to the original images you're enhancing.

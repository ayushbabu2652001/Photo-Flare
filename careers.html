Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer remote work options?

Yes! Many of our positions are available for remote work. We're a distributed team with members across multiple time zones. We specify in each job posting whether it's remote-friendly, hybrid, or location-specific.

What's the company culture like?

We're a collaborative, innovative team that values transparency, ownership, and user-centric design. We believe in giving our team members autonomy while providing support when needed. Regular team-building activities and company-wide meetings help maintain our strong culture even in a distributed environment.

Do you provide relocation assistance?

For roles that require specific locations, we offer comprehensive relocation packages that include moving expenses, temporary housing, and assistance with visa processes where applicable.

What does career growth look like at Photo Flare?

We're committed to helping our team members grow professionally. We offer regular performance reviews, clear promotion pathways, mentorship programs, and dedicated learning budgets. Many of our senior leaders started in individual contributor roles and grew with the company.

Ready to Join Our Team?

We're always looking for passionate, talented individuals who want to make an impact. If you don't see the perfect role but believe you'd be a great fit, we'd still love to hear from you.

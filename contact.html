Common Contact Questions

What's the best way to get technical support?

For technical issues, we recommend using our live chat during business hours for immediate assistance. If your issue is complex or requires file analysis, please use the contact form and attach relevant files (screenshots, error messages, etc.).

How long does it take to get a response?

Response times vary by contact method:

  • Live Chat: Immediate during business hours (9AM-6PM EST)
  • Email/Contact Form: Within 24 hours on business days
  • Billing Inquiries: Within 48 hours
Do you offer phone support?

Yes, we offer phone support for premium subscribers. Free users can reach us via email, live chat, or the contact form. Premium users can schedule a callback through their account dashboard or call our support line directly.

Can I request a new feature?

Absolutely! We welcome feature requests from all users. Please use the contact form and select "Feature Request" as the subject. Our product team reviews all suggestions and the most popular ones often get implemented in future updates.

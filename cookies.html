What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

At Photo Flare, we use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use different types of cookies for various purposes:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Required for basic site functionality and security Session / 1 year
Functional Remember your preferences and settings 1 year
Analytics Help us understand how visitors interact with our website 2 years
Advertising Used to deliver relevant advertisements 90 days

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Photo Flare without accepting cookies?

Essential cookies are required for our website to function properly. Without them, certain features may not work correctly. However, you can disable non-essential cookies using our cookie controls.

How often is this cookie policy updated?

We review and update our Cookie Policy annually or whenever there are significant changes to our cookie practices. We encourage you to check this page periodically for the latest information.

Do you use third-party cookies?

Yes, we use some third-party services that may place cookies on your device. These include analytics services like Google Analytics and advertising networks. We ensure that all third-party services comply with relevant privacy regulations.

How can I delete cookies from my browser?

You can delete cookies through your browser settings. The process varies by browser, but generally, you can find cookie controls in the "Privacy" or "Security" sections of your browser settings. You can also use our cookie controls above to manage your preferences for our website specifically.

Have Questions About Our Cookie Policy?

If you have any questions or concerns about how we use cookies, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I edit text in any PDF?

Yes, our PDF editor can edit text in most PDF files. For scanned documents, you'll need our AI-powered OCR feature (available in Pro) to make text editable.

Is my PDF secure when editing?

Absolutely! All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your computer and are not stored on our servers.

What is the maximum file size for editing?

Free users can edit PDF files up to 10MB in size. Premium users can edit files up to 100MB (monthly) or 500MB (yearly plan).

Can I edit PDFs on mobile devices?

Yes, our PDF editor is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets.

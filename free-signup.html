Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Photo Flare plans and billing

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and in some regions, Apple Pay and Google Pay.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access to Pro features until the end of your billing period.
Is there a free trial for Pro features?
We don't offer a free trial, but we have a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with Photo Flare Pro, we'll refund your payment.
Do you offer discounts for students or nonprofits?
Yes, we offer a 25% discount for students and registered nonprofits. Please contact our support team with proof of status to get your discount code.
Can I switch between monthly and yearly plans?
Absolutely! You can switch between monthly and yearly plans at any time. When you switch, the new billing cycle will start immediately.
How secure is my data with Photo Flare?
Your privacy and security are our top priorities. All file processing happens locally in your browser - your files never leave your computer. We don't store any of your documents or images on our servers.

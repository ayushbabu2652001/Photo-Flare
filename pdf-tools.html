Frequently Asked Questions

Are my PDF files secure?

Yes, absolutely. All PDF processing happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your computer and are not stored on our servers.

Is there a file size limit?

Free users can process PDF files up to 10MB. Pro users can process files up to 500MB depending on their subscription plan.

Do I need to install any software?

No installation required. All our PDF tools work directly in your web browser on any device.

What PDF formats are supported?

We support all standard PDF formats including PDF/A for archiving and encrypted PDFs.

Can I use these tools on mobile devices?

Yes, our PDF tools are fully responsive and work perfectly on smartphones and tablets.

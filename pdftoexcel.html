Convert PDF to Editable Excel Spreadsheets Online

Our advanced PDF to Excel converter transforms your PDF documents into fully editable Microsoft Excel spreadsheets with exceptional accuracy. Whether you need to extract financial data, convert reports for analysis, or migrate tabular data, our tool delivers professional results.

Unlike basic converters, our tool intelligently analyzes document structure to detect tables, headers, and data relationships. The AI-powered engine preserves complex table layouts, numerical formatting, and data types, ensuring your converted spreadsheet maintains the integrity of the original data.

All file processing happens securely in your browser, ensuring your confidential financial data never leaves your computer. We support batch conversion for multiple files and offer flexible output options including XLSX, XLS, CSV, and ODS formats.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the PDF to Excel conversion?

Our converter achieves over 95% accuracy for well-structured PDF tables, with excellent preservation of data formatting and relationships. For complex tables with merged cells or irregular layouts, the accuracy may vary but generally remains high thanks to our advanced detection algorithms.

Can I convert scanned PDF documents to Excel?

Yes, our premium OCR feature can extract tabular data from scanned PDF documents. This requires a Pro subscription as it uses advanced AI technology to recognize text in images and reconstruct table structures while preserving data relationships.

What Excel formats are supported?

We support XLSX (Microsoft Excel 2007 and later), XLS (Excel 97-2003), CSV (Comma Separated Values), and ODS (OpenDocument Spreadsheet) formats. The XLSX format is recommended for best compatibility and feature support with modern versions of Excel.

Does the converter preserve formulas and calculations?

Yes, our advanced formula detection (available in Pro) can identify common mathematical operations and convert them to Excel formulas. However, complex custom formulas may need manual verification after conversion.

Can I convert only specific tables or data ranges?

Yes, our tool allows you to select specific pages, tables, or data ranges for conversion. You can also use the manual table detection feature (available in Pro) to precisely control which data is extracted and how it's organized in the Excel file.

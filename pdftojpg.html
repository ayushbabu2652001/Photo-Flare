Frequently Asked Questions

What is the maximum resolution for converted JPG images?

Our converter supports resolutions up to 300 DPI (dots per inch) for high-quality output. For most use cases, 150-200 DPI provides excellent quality while keeping file sizes manageable. Higher resolutions are available for professional printing needs.

Can I convert specific pages or page ranges?

Yes, you can convert all pages, specific page ranges, or individual pages from your PDF document. The tool provides flexible page selection options to extract exactly the content you need.

What image formats are supported besides JPG?

We support multiple output formats: JPG (recommended for photographs), PNG (lossless format for graphics), WebP (modern format with excellent compression), and BMP (uncompressed format for maximum quality).

How does the AI image enhancement work?

Our AI enhancement (available in Pro) analyzes each image to improve sharpness, reduce noise, optimize colors, and enhance details. It's particularly effective for scanned documents and low-quality PDFs, significantly improving the visual quality of converted images.

Can I process multiple PDF files at once?

Yes, our batch processing feature (available in Pro) allows you to convert multiple PDF files to JPG images simultaneously. You can also choose to download all converted images as a single ZIP archive for convenience.

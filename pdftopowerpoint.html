Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the PDF to PowerPoint conversion?

Our converter achieves over 90% accuracy for presentation PDFs, with excellent preservation of layouts, images, and text formatting. For complex designs with multiple columns or intricate graphics, the accuracy may vary but generally remains high thanks to our advanced detection algorithms.

Can I convert scanned PDF presentations to PowerPoint?

Yes, our premium OCR feature can extract text and layout from scanned PDF presentations. This requires a Pro subscription as it uses advanced AI technology to recognize text in images and reconstruct the presentation structure while preserving the original design.

What PowerPoint formats are supported?

We support PPTX (Microsoft PowerPoint 2007 and later), PPT (PowerPoint 97-2003), and PDF slide formats. The PPTX format is recommended for best compatibility and feature support with modern versions of PowerPoint.

Does the converter preserve animations and transitions?

PDF files don't contain animation or transition data, so these elements cannot be preserved during conversion. However, the static content, layout, and design of each slide are accurately converted, providing an excellent foundation that you can then enhance with animations in PowerPoint.

Can I convert only specific pages to slides?

Yes, our tool allows you to select specific page ranges for conversion. You can also use the manual slide detection feature (available in Pro) to precisely control which pages become slides and how content is distributed across slides.

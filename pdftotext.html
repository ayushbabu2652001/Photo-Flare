Convert PDF to Editable Text Online

Our advanced PDF to Text converter transforms your PDF documents into fully editable text files with exceptional accuracy. Whether you need to extract content from reports, convert scanned documents, or migrate text data, our tool delivers professional results.

Unlike basic converters, our tool intelligently analyzes document structure to preserve formatting, detect tables, and maintain text relationships. The AI-powered OCR engine can extract text from even the most challenging scanned documents, with accuracy rates exceeding 95% for most document types.

All file processing happens securely in your browser, ensuring your confidential documents never leave your computer. We support batch conversion for multiple files and offer flexible output options including plain text, formatted text, HTML, Markdown, and Word documents.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the PDF to Text conversion?

Our converter achieves over 99% accuracy for digital PDFs and approximately 95-98% for scanned documents using advanced OCR technology. The accuracy varies based on document quality, font clarity, and language complexity, but generally remains very high thanks to our sophisticated text recognition algorithms.

Can I extract text from scanned PDF documents?

Yes, our premium OCR feature can extract text from scanned PDF documents and images. This requires a Pro subscription as it uses advanced AI technology to recognize text in images and convert it to editable content while maintaining formatting and structure.

What text formats are supported for output?

We support multiple output formats: Plain Text (.txt) for simple extraction, Formatted Text with layout preservation, HTML for web use, Markdown for documentation, and Word Document (.docx) for Microsoft Word compatibility. Each format maintains different levels of formatting and structure.

Does the converter preserve tables and formatting?

Yes, our advanced extraction options can preserve table structures, lists, headings, and basic formatting. For complex layouts, we offer structured extraction that maintains document hierarchy and relationships between text elements.

Can I convert password-protected PDFs?

Yes, our tool can extract text from password-protected PDFs if you provide the correct password. We do not support breaking or removing passwords from secured documents, as that would violate copyright and security protections.

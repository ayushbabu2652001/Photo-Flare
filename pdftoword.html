Convert PDF to Editable Word Documents Online

Our advanced PDF to Word converter uses cutting-edge technology to transform your PDF files into fully editable Microsoft Word documents. Whether you need to edit a contract, update a report, or extract content from a scanned document, our tool delivers exceptional accuracy and formatting preservation.

Unlike basic converters that simply extract text, our tool intelligently analyzes document structure, preserving tables, images, headers, footers, and complex layouts. The AI-powered OCR engine can even extract text from scanned documents and images, making previously uneditable content fully manipulable.

All file processing happens securely in your browser, ensuring your sensitive documents never leave your computer. We support batch conversion for multiple files and offer various output formats including DOCX, DOC, RTF, and ODT to meet all your compatibility needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the PDF to Word conversion?

Our converter achieves over 95% accuracy for text-based PDFs and approximately 85-90% for scanned documents using advanced OCR technology. Formatting preservation is excellent for most document types, with tables, images, and complex layouts maintained accurately.

Can I convert scanned PDFs to Word?

Yes, our premium OCR feature can extract text from scanned PDFs and images. This requires a Pro subscription as it uses advanced AI technology to recognize text in images and convert it to editable content while preserving the original layout.

What is the maximum file size I can convert?

Free users can convert PDF files up to 10MB in size. Pro users enjoy a 100MB limit per file and can process multiple files simultaneously through batch conversion. For extremely large documents, we recommend splitting them into smaller sections.

Are my files secure when using this converter?

Absolutely. All conversion happens locally in your browser - your files are never uploaded to our servers. This ensures complete privacy and security for your sensitive documents. Once you close the browser tab, all temporary data is automatically deleted.

What Word formats are supported?

We support multiple output formats: DOCX (Microsoft Word 2007 and later), DOC (Word 97-2003), RTF (Rich Text Format compatible with most word processors), and ODT (OpenDocument Format used by LibreOffice and OpenOffice).

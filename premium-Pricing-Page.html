Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and subscription plans.

Can I change my plan later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll immediately gain access to the new features. When downgrading, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is there a free trial?
Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial for all premium plans. No credit card is required to start the trial, and you can cancel at any time during the trial period without being charged.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and in some regions, Apple Pay and Google Pay.
Do you offer discounts for students or nonprofits?
Yes, we offer a 50% discount for verified students and nonprofit organizations. Contact our support team with proof of status to get your discount applied to any paid plan.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access to premium features until the end of your billing cycle. We don't charge cancellation fees.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all annual plans. If you're not satisfied with our service, contact our support team within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and plans.

Can I change plans anytime?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to the new features. When downgrading, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Do you offer discounts for students or nonprofits?
Yes, we offer a 50% discount for verified students and nonprofit organizations. Contact our support team with proof of status to get your discount applied to any paid plan.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and in some regions, Apple Pay and Google Pay. For enterprise plans, we also accept bank transfers.
Is there a free trial?
Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial for all paid plans. No credit card is required to start the trial. You can cancel anytime during the trial period without being charged.

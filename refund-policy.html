Guarantee FAQs

Common questions about our money-back guarantee

What if I change my mind after 30 days?
Our money-back guarantee applies only within the first 30 days of your initial purchase. After 30 days, we don't offer refunds for change of mind, but you can cancel your subscription at any time to prevent future charges.
How long does it take to receive my refund?
We process refund requests within 1-2 business days. Once processed, it typically takes 3-5 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your payment method and financial institution.
Do I need to provide a reason for my refund?
No, we operate a "no questions asked" policy. You don't need to explain why you're requesting a refund. We believe you're the best judge of whether Photo Flare Pro meets your needs.
Does the guarantee apply to free trials?
We don't currently offer free trials for our Pro plans. Our 30-day money-back guarantee serves as your risk-free trial period. If you're not satisfied, you get a full refund.
Can I get a refund if I purchased through the Apple App Store or Google Play?
For purchases made through third-party app stores, refunds must be processed through those platforms according to their policies. Our direct website purchases are covered by our 30-day guarantee.

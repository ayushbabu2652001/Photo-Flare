Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

The free version includes basic tools with limitations (10MB file size, ads, slower processing). Pro unlocks all 100+ tools, removes file size limits, provides ad-free experience, and offers priority processing.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access to Pro features until the end of your billing period.

Is there a free trial for Pro features?

We don't offer a free trial, but we provide a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with Pro, you can request a full refund within 30 days.

How secure is my data with Photo Flare?

Very secure. All file processing happens in your browser, and files are never stored on our servers. For Pro users, we use additional encryption for enhanced security.

Can I upgrade from monthly to yearly?

Yes, you can upgrade at any time. When you upgrade, we'll prorate the remaining time on your monthly plan and apply it to your yearly subscription.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will rotating PDF pages affect the document quality?

No, rotating PDF pages with our tool does not affect the quality of your document. We use lossless rotation techniques that preserve the original quality of text, images, and vector graphics in your PDF.

How does the AI auto-rotation feature work?

Our AI analyzes the content of each page, including text direction, image orientation, and layout patterns. It then determines the correct reading orientation and automatically rotates pages that are upside down or sideways to ensure optimal readability.

Can I rotate only specific pages in a PDF?

Yes, our individual page rotation mode allows you to select specific pages and apply different rotation angles to each one. You can also use batch rotation for groups of pages or the entire document.

Is there a file size limit for PDF rotation?

Free users can rotate PDF files up to 50MB. Premium users have access to larger file sizes up to 500MB, depending on their subscription plan.

Can I use this tool on mobile devices?

Yes, our PDF rotation tool is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones and tablets. The interface adapts to your screen size for optimal usability.

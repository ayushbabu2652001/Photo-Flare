How Your Data Is Processed

A transparent look at what happens to your files when you use Photo Flare

File Upload

You select a file from your device. The file remains in your browser's memory and is never transmitted to our servers.

Local Processing

Our tools process your file directly in your browser using JavaScript. All operations happen locally on your device.

Result Download

The processed file is available for download directly to your device. We don't store any copies of your files.

Automatic Cleanup

When you leave the page or complete your task, all file data is automatically cleared from your browser's memory.

Security FAQs

Common questions about our security practices and privacy protections

Do you store my files on your servers?
No, we do not store your files on our servers. All file processing happens locally in your browser. The only exception is if you explicitly use our cloud storage feature (available to Pro users), and even then, files are encrypted and can be deleted at any time.
Is my data encrypted during processing?
Since all processing happens locally in your browser, your data doesn't need to be encrypted during processing because it never leaves your device. For cloud storage features, we use industry-standard AES-256 encryption both in transit and at rest.
Do you collect any personal information?
We collect minimal anonymous usage statistics to improve our services, but we never collect personal identifying information or the content of your files. For Pro users, we only collect necessary billing information through secure payment processors.
How do you ensure the security of your website?
We use HTTPS with modern TLS encryption for all connections, regularly update our dependencies to patch security vulnerabilities, conduct security audits, and follow industry best practices for web application security.
What happens if I close the browser during processing?
If you close the browser during processing, any temporary data in memory is immediately cleared. Since we don't store your files on our servers, there's no risk of data lingering after your session ends.

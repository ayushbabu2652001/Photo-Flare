Frequently Asked Questions

Are AI-generated signatures legally binding?

Yes, in most jurisdictions, digital signatures created with our tool are legally binding, provided they meet certain criteria such as intent to sign, consent to do business electronically, and association of the signature with the record. Our signatures include cryptographic verification to ensure authenticity.

How does the AI signature generation work?

Our AI analyzes thousands of handwriting samples to understand stylistic patterns. When you provide your name or draw a sample, the AI creates a unique signature that maintains consistency with real handwriting while ensuring it's distinct and personalized to you.

Is my signature data secure?

Absolutely. All signature processing happens locally in your browser. Your signature data never leaves your device, and we don't store any of your personal information or documents on our servers.

Can I use this tool on mobile devices?

Yes, our PDF signing tool is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones and tablets. You can draw your signature directly on the touchscreen or use the AI generation features.

What file formats are supported?

We support all standard PDF formats. The maximum file size for free users is 50MB, while premium users can process files up to 500MB.
